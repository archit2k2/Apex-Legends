Arenas are the underground, unsanctioned fight clubs on the Arenas. Fight to the finish in this action-packed 3v3 round-based game mode. Pick your loadout, and sharpen your skills on bespoke maps where there's no room for luck.
Arena Trailer Gameplay Maps
It's just your squad and their squad. The team who survives, wins.
The first squad to win three rounds (with a two round margin) is victorious. If the squads are deadlocked in a 4-4 tie, the match goes to sudden death.
Pick your loadout each round and earn and spend Crafting Materials to improve your options between rounds. Your equipment doesn't carry over from round to round, but Crafting Materials can be saved up to purchase better equipment in later rounds.
Arenas features permanent bespoke maps like Party Crasher and Phase Runner, as well as weekly rotating maps. While each varies in size, they're designed to encourage frequent firefights and constant action. No hiding and hoping someone else will do your dirty work! Get More Details
